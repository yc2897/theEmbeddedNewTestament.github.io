## 🎯 **Success Tips**

### **Before the Interview**
- **Review Fundamentals**: Ensure solid understanding of core concepts
- **Practice Coding**: Solve problems on paper/whiteboard
- **Research Company**: Understand their technology stack and focus areas
- **Prepare Questions**: Have thoughtful questions about the role and company

### **During the Interview**
- **Think Aloud**: Explain your thought process as you solve problems
- **Ask Clarifying Questions**: Ensure you understand the problem requirements
- **Start Simple**: Begin with basic solutions, then optimize
- **Be Honest**: Admit when you don't know something, but show willingness to learn

### **After the Interview**
- **Reflect**: Identify areas for improvement
- **Follow Up**: Send thank-you notes and any additional information
- **Learn**: Use the experience to improve your preparation
